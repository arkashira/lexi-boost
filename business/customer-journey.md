- User encounters a problem with the accuracy and efficiency of their current natural language processing (NLP) solution.
- User searches for solutions to improve NLP capabilities.

### Friction Points
- Lack of awareness about the existence of lexi-boost.
- Difficulty understanding the unique value proposition of lexi-boost compared to other NLP solutions.

### User Emotions
- Frustration with current NLP limitations.
- Curiosity about potential solutions.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise explanation of lexi-boost's unique value proposition.
- Easy access to detailed product information and demos.

### Metric
- Impressions on targeted ads and organic search results.
