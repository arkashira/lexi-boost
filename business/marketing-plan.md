# Marketing Plan for Lexi-Boost

## Positioning
**Primary:** "Lexi-Boost: Precision Language Models for Developers"
**Alternatives:**
1. "Enhance Your NLP: Lexi-Boost for Accurate Language Models"
2. "Lexi-Boost: The Developer's Edge in Natural Language Processing"
3. "Supercharge Your AI: Lexi-Boost for Flawless Language Models"

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
1. **Alex the AI Developer**
   - **Daily Job:** Builds and deploys AI models for enterprise clients.
   - **Budget:** $5,000 - $10,000/month for AI tools and enhancements.
2. **Jamie the Data Scientist**
   - **Daily Job:** Analyzes large datasets and integrates NLP models.
   - **Budget:** $3,000 - $7,000/month for data and AI tools.
3. **Taylor the Product Manager**
   - **Daily Job:** Oversees AI product development and integration.
   - **Budget:** $2,000 - $5,000/month for product enhancement tools.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 - $300 per customer acquisition.
2. **Tech Conferences and Webinars**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $150 - $250 per customer acquisition.
3. **Google Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $100 - $200 per customer acquisition.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Introduction to Lexi-Boost, benefits, and use cases.
- **Week 5-8:** Case studies, technical deep dives, and developer testimonials.
- **Week 9-12:** Advanced features, integration guides, and success stories.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize marketing materials, set up ad campaigns, and prepare launch event.
- **D-0:** Official launch on product website, social media announcement, and press release.
- **D+30:** First customer testimonials, initial performance metrics, and feedback collection.
- **D+90:** Expand marketing efforts based on initial success, introduce new features, and scale customer acquisition.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Aim for 500 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR Target by D+90:** $50,000 in Monthly Recurring Revenue.
